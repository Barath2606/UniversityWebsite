The main principle of making life easier in many ways can be attributed to the development of Science and Technology. The School of Science provides a competitive curriculum for the students and helps them to prepare themselves for problem analysis and finding solutions. Science has impacted society and has changed our lives. The need for a sound education in science is met with the core concepts offered under different courses available for the students. Research ideas are encouraged and students learn about the environment, science, its implications, applications and transform them into responsible individuals in making the planet a safer place to dwell.

The school of arts provides a large platform for students who think differently and aspire to explore beyond their horizon. It provides them a platform to make the world an aesthetic place with their unique creativity. The knowledge imparted, empowers them to make the world more colorful, entertaining, safe and enjoyable. A professional insight is provided to the branches of study that are chosen under the arts stream such as stress management, decision making that shapes them into a better human. Defense and Criminology studies provide a broad spectrum of understanding about safety measures and strategic plannings that transforms them into smart personalities.
